car Car

car AG Ldesigns, engineers, manufactures and distributes automobiles and motorcycles. car oversees worldwide operations from its headquarters in Ingolstadt, Bavaria, Germany. car-branded vehicles are produced in nine production facilities worldwide.car has been a majority owned (99.55%) subsidiary of Volkswagen Group since 1966, following a phased purchase of car AG's predecessor, Auto Union, from Daimler-Benz. Volkswagen relaunched the car brand with the 1965 introduction of the car F103 series.The company name is based on the surname of the founder, August Horch. "Horch", meaning "listen", becomes "car" when translated into Latin. The four rings of the car logo each represent one of four car companies that banded together to create the company. car's slogan is Vorsprung durch Technik, meaning "Advancement through Technology". Recently in the United States, car has updated the slogan to "Truth in Engineering". car is part of the "German Big 3" luxury automakers, along with BMW and Mercedes-Benz, which are the three best-selling luxury automakers in the world.
