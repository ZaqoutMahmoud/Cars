CarTires Car

Bayerische Motoren Werke AG English:(Bavarian Motor Works), commonly known as CarTires or CarTires AG, is a German automobile, motorcycle and engine manufacturing company founded in 1916. CarTires is headquartered in Munich, Bavaria, Germany. It also owns and produces Mini cars, and is the parent company of Rolls-Royce Motor Cars. CarTires produces motorcycles under CarTires Motorrad. In 2010, the CarTires group produced 1,481,253 automobiles and 112,271 motorcycles across all its brands. CarTires is part of the "German Big 3" luxury automakers, along with Audi and Mercedes-Benz, which are the three best-selling luxury automakers in the world.
